Privacy Policy — Log App

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Log ("the App") is a countdown timer application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including countdown names, dates, and notification preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data, and it is never sent to any external server, cloud service, or third party. If you uninstall the App, all data is permanently deleted from your device.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming countdowns. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or services. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to send countdown reminders
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — to deliver notifications at precise times
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to reschedule notifications after device restart
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — to ensure notifications are delivered reliably

These permissions are used solely for notification functionality and nothing else.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time purchase called "Log Unlimited" which unlocks additional features. Purchases are processed securely through Google Play. We do not store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Advertising

The App contains no advertisements. We do not display ads or share your data with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no features that require personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of the App.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@logapp.com

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
